Feature of eZee ver 10.1


eZee ver. 10.1 is an Accounting ERP application developed to fulfill the key requirements of fruit and vegetable merchants in India. It Cover whole accounting requirements of above said users. Covers all accounting transactions like customers accounts, Suppliers Accounts, voucher entry, ledgers, customer out standings, supplier outstandings, sale and purchase registers ,profit and loss reports, Crate management ,wataks and loading.


1: eZee ver 10.1 is a multiuser ERP application or can run on individual computer also.

2: Have ability to prints reports on laser printer or in DOS format in continuous stationary in dot-matrix printers.

3: eZee ver 10.1 is very easy to operate it have three modules that are as under:-

  • If the customer need only to operate customers bills and crate its separate module.
  • If the customer need only to operate customers bills, crate, wataks and loading its seprate module.
  • Full accounting software include all the features of sabzi Mandi software along with the financial accounting have different module.
